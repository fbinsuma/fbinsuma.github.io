ETA Tool

UI/UX designer and web developer

The ETA Tool all began when me and 3 of my classmates joining the Calgary Hackathon 2019. The hackathon was two days event, and the topic was pedestrian safety. We came up with the idea of creating a tool that would bring the City of Calgary to Vision Zero goal. The web tool visualized to the end user that speed reduction can have minimal affect on your commute but can saves many lives as it can reduce the force of collision. After creating a demo tool for the hackathon, the City of Calgary contact us to develop the tool as the city was looking for an app that can help both the City engineers and the public to visualize those speed reduction changes.
or this tool we created a website using JavaScript, HTML and CSS so it can be accessible to as many people. We also use GIS to compile and compress the city data to have a fast user experience.

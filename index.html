ETA Tool

UI/UX designer and web developer

The ETA Tool all began when me and 3 of my classmates joing the Calgary Hackathon 2019. The hackthone was two days event and the topic was padestrain safety. We came up with the idea of creating a tool that would bring teh City of Calgary to Vision Zero goal. The web tool visualizaed to the end user that speed reduction can have minial affect on your cmmute but can saves many lives as it can reduce the force of collision. After creating a demo tool for the hacthone the City of Calgary contact us to develope the tool as the City was looking for an app that can help both the CIty engineers and the public to visiualize those speed reduction changes.
For this tool we created a website using Javascript, HTML and CSS so it can be accessible to as many people. We also use GIS to compile and compress the city data to have a fast user experince.

About Me
